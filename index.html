A Visionary Legacy, a Modern Evolution

Canadian Multitek Engineering Corp. (C.M.E.) was founded in 1989 by the late **Eng. Ahmed Zarrug**, with a specialized focus on the highly-regulated aviation industry. His vision brought Canadian precision to global travel's essential systems.

Our initial core competency was in the implementation and integration of **VOR (VHF Omnidirectional Range)** and **DME (Distance Measuring Equipment)** navigation systems, ensuring the absolute reliability of international flight corridors.

Today, C.M.E. honors Eng. Zarrug’s legacy by operating as a comprehensive multidisciplinary consultancy, applying our heritage of precision and safety to complex global challenges across multiple sectors.
