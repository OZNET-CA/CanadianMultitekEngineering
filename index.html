A Visionary Legacy

Founded by the late **Eng. Ahmed Zarrug**, Canadian Multitek Engineering Corp. was established in 1989 with a mission to bring high-precision aerospace standards to the engineering landscape.

What began as a specialized firm for VOR and DME airport navigation systems has evolved into a global multidisciplinary powerhouse. We bridge the gap between technical complexity and practical infrastructure solutions.
