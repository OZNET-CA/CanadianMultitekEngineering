Engineering heritage. Global ambition.

Canadian engineering excellence for global infrastructure, aviation, and complex technical projects.

Canadian Multitek Engineering Corp. was founded in 1989 by the late Engineer Ahmed Zarrug. CME began with a specialized focus on VOR, DME, and aircraft and airport navigation systems, and has since evolved into a broader engineering and technical advisory enterprise positioned to support international clients with Canadian professionalism, disciplined execution, and high technical standards.

From aviation infrastructure to buildings, utilities, intelligent systems, project advisory, and multidisciplinary engineering support, CME is positioned to help clients advance important projects with confidence, structure, and a clear commitment to quality.

Our story

Built on technical precision and positioned for disciplined international delivery.

CME was established on a foundation of disciplined engineering thinking. Its original focus on airport and navigation system environments shaped a culture centered on reliability, coordination, safety-conscious decision-making, and respect for mission-critical performance.

Today, CME presents a broader vision: to connect global clients with Canadian engineering quality, practical technical leadership, and the right multidisciplinary capability for projects that demand professionalism and confidence. Our aim is to support public and private sector partners with engineering, advisory, and infrastructure-related services that reflect the credibility, discipline, and seriousness expected from a Canadian firm.

CME is structured to pursue opportunities across multiple engineering domains through qualified professionals, trusted specialist relationships, and delivery models tailored to each client environment and jurisdiction.
