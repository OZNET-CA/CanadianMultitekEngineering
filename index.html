A Visionary Legacy

Canadian Multitek Engineering Corp. (C.M.E.) was founded in 1989 by the late **Eng. Ahmed Zarrug**, establishing a standard of precision in the aviation industry.

Our roots in **VOR and DME navigation systems** laid the groundwork for a firm that values technical accuracy above all else. Today, we apply that same aerospace rigor to multidisciplinary engineering challenges worldwide.

We continue to honor the legacy of Eng. Zarrug by delivering engineering solutions that meet the highest Canadian and International standards.
